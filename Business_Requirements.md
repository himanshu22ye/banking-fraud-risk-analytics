# Banking Fraud & Risk Analytics Platform

## 1. Project Overview

Financial institutions process millions of transactions every day. Detecting suspicious financial activities is essential for reducing financial crime, ensuring regulatory compliance, and protecting customers.

This project analyzes banking transaction data to identify suspicious transaction patterns, assess account risk, and develop an interactive analytics dashboard for business stakeholders.

---

## 2. Business Problem

Banks require a data-driven solution to monitor transaction activity and identify suspicious behavior that could indicate money laundering or financial fraud.

The objective is to transform raw transaction data into actionable business insights that support decision-making and regulatory compliance.
